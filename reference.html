FAQ

Good questions.

Is Etiqa medical advice? +
No. Etiqa is an informational product-evaluation tool. Scores reflect nutritional data and established methodology — they are not diagnoses, medical recommendations, or a substitute for professional dietary guidance.
How are scores calculated? +
Etiqa uses an adapted version of Nutri-Score v2 — a research-backed European food scoring system. Scores weigh negative factors (sugar, saturated fat, sodium, trans fat) against positive ones (fiber, protein, micronutrients) per 100g of product. No brand pays to influence their score.
What if a product I search for is missing? +
Our Panama database is actively growing. If a product is missing, it goes on our priority list for the next data update. You can also flag missing products directly in the app.
Does Etiqa take money from food brands? +
Never. Etiqa is funded by users (Etiqa Plus subscriptions) and aligned investors — not food companies. We have no commercial relationship with any brand in our database and no brand can pay to improve their score.
Is it available in Spanish? +
Yes — fully. Every screen, score explanation, ingredient flag, and alternative is available in both English and Spanish. The app detects your device language and defaults accordingly.
What is Etiqa Plus? +
Etiqa Plus is our premium tier. It unlocks dietary filters (gluten-free, no added sugar, high protein), your full health trend dashboard, unlimited bookmarks, and offline mode. The core search and scoring experience is always free.
