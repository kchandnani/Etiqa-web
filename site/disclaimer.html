Etiqa provides product scores, nutrition summaries, and comparisons for general informational purposes. Nothing in the Service constitutes a medical diagnosis, treatment recommendation, or professional healthcare opinion. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making dietary changes, especially if you have allergies, intolerances, chronic conditions, or are pregnant or nursing.

2. Algorithmic scores — not certifications

Scores are algorithmic estimates generated from publicly available nutrition data using adapted Nutri-Score v2 methodology. They are not government-issued certifications, endorsements, or official quality ratings. Scores reflect the data available at the time of computation and may not account for recent product reformulations.

3. Data accuracy & completeness

While we strive for accuracy, product data may be incomplete, outdated, or contain errors. Nutrition information is sourced from publicly available databases and product labels, which may change without notice. You should always verify information against the actual product packaging before making decisions.

Cross-contamination risks, trace ingredients, and regional formulation differences may not be reflected in our data. If you have food allergies or sensitivities, always check the physical product label and consult your healthcare provider.
