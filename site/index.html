FAQ

Is Etiqa a medical app?
No. Etiqa is an informational product-evaluation tool.

Are scores final medical truth?
No. Scores are algorithmic estimates based on available data and can change as coverage improves.

What if a product is missing?
US Beta coverage is expanding. Missing products help us prioritize what to add next.

Can I use Etiqa without an account?
Yes. Core product exploration is guest-friendly.
