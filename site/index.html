Our mission

We believe everyone deserves clear food information before buying — not after.

Etiqa does not accept brand advertising to influence product scoring. Our goal is independent, understandable guidance for everyday shoppers.

Why Panama first

We are starting with Panama-specific coverage and quality, then expanding across LATAM with the same local-first standard.

FAQ

Is Etiqa medical advice?
No. Etiqa is an informational product-evaluation tool.

How are scores generated?
Scores are algorithmic estimates based on available product data and can improve as coverage and validation improve.

What if a product is missing?
Panama coverage is expanding quickly. Missing products help us prioritize ingestion and verification.

Do brands pay to influence rankings?
No. Product scoring is independent and not sold to advertisers.
